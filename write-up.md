# Daily Reflection Tree — Write-up  
**Name:** Prince Pravin Maurya  

---

## 1. Introduction  

This project is a deterministic reflection system designed to help users review their day in a structured way. The goal is to improve self-awareness by guiding users through a fixed sequence of questions and reflections.

Unlike AI-based systems, this tool does not rely on dynamic responses. Instead, it uses predefined logic to ensure consistency, predictability, and reliability.

---

## 2. Design Approach  

The system is built as a decision tree where each question has fixed options, and each option leads to a specific path. This ensures that the same inputs always produce the same outputs.

Key design principles:
- Deterministic flow (no randomness)
- No AI usage at runtime
- Fixed options for clarity
- Simple and conversational language

---

## 3. The Three Axes of Reflection  

### Axis 1: Locus (Victim vs Victor)  
This axis explores whether users see themselves in control of their actions or influenced by external factors.

### Axis 2: Contribution (Contribution vs Entitlement)  
This axis focuses on whether users think about what they contributed or what they expected to receive.

### Axis 3: Perspective (Self vs Others)  
This axis examines whether users focus on themselves or consider the impact on others.

---

## 4. Question Design Strategy  

The questions are designed to reflect real-life thinking patterns. Options are realistic and non-judgmental, helping users reflect naturally.

---

## 5. Branching Logic  

The system uses decision nodes to route users based on their responses. Each path leads to a specific reflection.

Signals track:
- Axis 1 → Internal vs External  
- Axis 2 → Contribution vs Entitlement  
- Axis 3 → Self vs Others  

---

## 6. Deterministic Design  

All responses are predefined. The system does not use AI at runtime, ensuring consistent and reliable outputs.

---

## 7. Conclusion  

This system translates psychological concepts into a structured reflection tool. It helps users understand their actions, contributions, and perspective in a simple and consistent way.
